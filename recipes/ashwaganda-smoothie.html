Ashwagandha Smoothie

Description

This delicious ashwagandha banana smoothie is the perferct addition to your morning routine. Ashwagandha powder hails from India, where it has long been used to help soothe anxiety.

Nuts and milk make this protein packed smoothie a great way to start your day. Cinnamon and honey give it a bit of warmth and sweetness. It all comes together in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon ashwagandha powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 cup nuts or seeds
  • dash of honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
