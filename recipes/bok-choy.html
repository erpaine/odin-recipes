Stir-Fried Bok Choy

Description

This quick and easy bok choy is healthy and delicious. Garlic, ginger and soy sauce bring this popular green to life.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp avocado or vegetable oil
  • 1 lb baby bok choy
  • 1 tbsp garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • dash of red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Wash the baby bok choy thoroughly.
  2. Depending on the size, cut the bok choy into halves or quarters.
  3. Add the oil to a wok or skillet set over medium high heat.
  4. When the oil is hot, add the garlic and ginger and saute for 1 minute.
  5. Add the bok choy, soy sauce, and sesame oil and cook for two minutes.
  6. Add 1/2 cub water, cover and steam for 5 minutes or until the water has evaporated.
  7. Stiry fry uncovered for another minute or so, adding oil if necessary to prevent sticking.
  8. Top with red pepper flakes and serve hot.
